INTRODUCTION

The Private Office His Highness Sheikh Hamdan bin Ahmed Al Maktoum worked closely with business companies and regulatory bodies to find investment opportunities that enhance the business environment in Dubai and create the optimum economic conditions, and this cooperation has prompted the United Arab Emirates to take effective steps in proposing initiatives that will create A creative and accessible economic system that allows businesses to thrive and grow.
